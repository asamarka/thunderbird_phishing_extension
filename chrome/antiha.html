Contests & Competitions

►Innovate FPGA - Start the next Design Revolution! Sponsored by Terasic and Intel. Register by 12/1/17.
For the last 14 years, Terasic has held the Innovate Asia Contest and has inspired countless future engineers to design, to create, and innovate! The Innovate FPGA Design Contest is a global stage for teams to invent the future of embedded compute with Terasic and Intel. The competition is open to any participants ranging from academia and students through industry. The contest will provide a platform to communicate, to exchange ideas, and to compete with teams from around the world. Teams will be able to showcase their creativity and innovation with actual results and real-world solutions. Eligible teams will receive aFREE DE10-Nano development Kit.
